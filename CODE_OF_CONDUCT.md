# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Whale to be an open, practical, and respectful project.

Contributors and maintainers should help make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but be concrete about problems.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and product decisions.
- Accept that maintainers may narrow scope or reject changes to keep the project coherent.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Sexual language or imagery in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, code, wiki edits, and other contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainer through the repository contact path. If no dedicated contact is listed yet, open a minimal public issue asking for a private contact route without posting sensitive details.
